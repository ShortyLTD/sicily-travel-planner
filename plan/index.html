7-Day East Coast: Volcanoes, Ruins & Baroque Towns

7 NIGHTS

Catania → Mount Etna crater → Taormina's clifftop theater → Syracuse's ancient harbor. The most popular route. Best for first-timers and history lovers.

5-Day West Coast: Food, Villages & Salt Ponds

5 NIGHTS

Palermo's markets → Erice's hilltop fortress → Trapani's pink salt pools → Cefalù's sandy beach. Best for food lovers, photographers, and smaller groups.

10-Day Full Island: All of Sicily in One Trip

10 NIGHTS

Palermo → Taormina → Mount Etna → Syracuse → Valley of the Temples. Everything, zero regrets. Best for first-timers with time to spare.
