Plan your Sicily trip in the right order

Most people start with flights. That is backwards. Start with how long you have, pick your coast, choose a base, then book. This planner walks you through it step by step.

The four decisions that shape your trip
Step 1 — Route
How long should I spend?
Under 5 days, stay focused. At 7, pick one coast. At 10+, rent a car and do the full loop.
Step 2 — Base
Where should I base?
Taormina for scenery, Catania for access, Palermo for food, Ortigia for culture, Cefalu for beach.
Step 3 — Coast
East coast or west coast?
East is easier and more popular. West is deeper and more local. Under 7 days, pick one.
Step 4 — Transport
Do I need a car?
East coast cities: no. West coast or full island: yes. Short beach trip: maybe. We break it down.

How this planner works

Sicily Trip Planner is built to help you make real decisions, not to sell you on a destination you have already chosen. Every recommendation is based on practical travel experience, current transport connections, and honest cost estimates.

Recommendations follow a decision tree: route first, then base, then transport. This order prevents the most common planning mistakes.
Budget estimates use mid-range prices checked against current Booking.com and DiscoverCars listings. We update them when rates shift materially.
